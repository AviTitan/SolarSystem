Jupiter

  • Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System.
  • It is a gas giant with a mass more than two and a half times that of all the other planets in the Solar System combined, but slightly less than one-thousandth the mass of the Sun.
  • Jupiter is primarily composed of hydrogen, but helium comprises one quarter of its mass and one tenth of its volume
  • Surrounding Jupiter is a faint planetary ring system and a powerful magnetosphere.
