Sun

  • The Sun is the star at the center of the Solar System.
  • It is a nearly perfect sphere of hot plasma.
  • It formed approximately 4.6 billion years ago from the gravitational collapse of matter within a region of a large molecular cloud.
  • The Sun's core fuses about 600 million tons of hydrogen into helium every second, converting 4 million tons of matter into energy every second as a result.
